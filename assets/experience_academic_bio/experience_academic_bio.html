Biography

Manob Chandra Chanda is a Research Assistant and a fourth-year Information and Communication Engineering student at Pabna University of Science and Technology. His academic and research interests focus on artificial intelligence for medical image analysis and agriculture, with particular attention to deep learning-based problem solving. He has contributed to research on lung image segmentation using XR-U-Net and has also been involved in conference papers related to deepfake detection and speech emotion recognition. In addition to research publications and conference participation, he attended the Sakura Science Exchange Program in Japan, which expanded his international academic exposure and collaborative learning experience. Alongside his research activities, he continues to maintain strong academic performance and actively develop his skills in technical work, research writing, and presentation.
